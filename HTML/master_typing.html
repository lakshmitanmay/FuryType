Steps to Master Touch Typing

  1. Start Typing Using all 10 fingers.
  2. Place your left index finger on the f key and your right index finger on the j key
  3. Type without looking at the keyboard.
  4. Only look at the screen while typing
  5. Go to typingclub.com and start practising for an hour daily
  6. Remember, touch typing is not a destination, it's a journey. Keep practicing, and happy typing!

Shortcut keys to boost typing speed

Option + Delete
Allows deleting entire words

Command + Delete
Allows deleting everything at once
